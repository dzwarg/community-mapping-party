It's a Mapping Party!

Welcome to the Yarmouth, Maine Community Mapping Party!

Take your time to look around. This map shows the sidewalks and bike paths from the OpenStreetMap.org Planet file.

Want to add your own paths?

  1. Click on the "Locate" Control () in the upper right to record your location.
  2. Travel around and record your track.
  3. Click on the "Export" Control () to download your track as a GPX file.
