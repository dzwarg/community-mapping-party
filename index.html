Yarmouth Community Bike Map

The Yarmouth PTO, in collaboration with the Town of Yarmouth Bike and Pedestrian Committee, is working on an updated bike map to help students get to school safely.

  1. Click on the "Locate" Control () in the upper right to record your location.
  2. When you are done riding, click on the "Export" Control () to download your track as a GPX file.

  3. Email yarmouthPTO04096@gmail.com and attach your GPX file, and a local volunteer with the OpenStreetMap project will upload the data.

    Emails should be sent by parents, on behalf of participating students.
